Pocket Plants

Fall 2020 (WIP)

Summary

Pocket Plants is a 3rd Person survival game that takes place on a planet overrun with invasive plants. While beautiful, these plants produce too much carbon dioxide to sustain other life on the planet. The player must gather plants and utilize propagation to save their suffocating ecosystem.

Format

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Team Size: Seven

Development Time: 1 Semester

Roles and Tasks

Programmer: Programmed from plant functionality to wold completion system.

Design

Creating an interactive tutorial system helps player learn how to play the game as well as learning the narative behind the game

Ensuring that each level has its own complexity and requiring the player to complete the level within a time constraint adds another layer besides watering and leveling up the plants. Before each level was very linear and player can just water and level 1 or 2 plants to complete the level.
