Spooky Conga

July 28, 2018

Summary

Bring together the dance by uniting everyone and forming a conga line

Format

Game Engine: Unity 2D

Team Size: 5

Development Time: Two days over the course of a Game Jam

Design Goals

To create a game around the theme of "Repetitive".

Roles and Tasks

Lead Designer: Interesting level design.

Programmer: UI & menu functionality

Design Implementation

Spooky Conga is a snake like game where you are trying to lead a conga dance line at the hottest holloween party of the year.

This is my second project and I mostly spent my time learning and progressing as a game designer. I got more comfortable with Unity. The biggest struggle we had during this project was that the majority of team consisted of new aspiring game developers. As a group we learned a lot and all became better game designers and developers.
