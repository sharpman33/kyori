Leyline Knights

Spring 2021

Summary

Leyline Knights is a top-down 3D action roguelike game. Player controls a magic-wielding knight and climb the Great World Tree in search of powerful magic to help your sick sibling. Player battles through randomly ordered room encounters and acquire elemental upgrades to aid in their climb.

Format

Game Engine: Unreal Engine 4

Team Size: Sixteen

Development Time: 2 Semesters

Roles and Tasks

Tech Lead: Ensured all scripts produced by the team is at a certain quality

Programmer: Working on AI for enemies

Design

As tech lead, I oversee all programming tasks and ensure that all tasks are working properly and easily integrated for designers to use.

Working on AI, I began with first setting up enemy parenting structure is setup correctly and easily expandable in the future.

We decided to use BehaviorTrees and Blackboard.

Using BehaviorTree tasks allowed us to reuse task among mutiple enemies since some functinalities work between most enemies.
